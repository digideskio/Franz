ChunkedConsumer

Namespace: Franz.HighLevel

High level kafka consumer, consuming messages in chunks defined by MaxBytes, MinBytes and MaxWaitTime in the consumer options. Each call to the consume functions, will provide a new chunk of messages. If no messages are available an empty sequence will be returned.
